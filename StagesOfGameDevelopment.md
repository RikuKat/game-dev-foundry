# Additional External Resources

These resources are accessible overviews of important concepts that are invaluable for leaders to develop their leadership techniques and better understand the disciplines that make up their studio. They should not be considered comprehensive discipline coverage. 

## Books

### Leadership, Production, & Communication
- **Crucial Conversations: Tools for Talking When Stakes are High** by Kerry Patterson, Joseph Grenny, Ron McMillan, Al Switzler
- **Creativity, Inc.: Overcoming the Unseen Forces That Stand in the Way of True Inspiration** by Ed Catmull
- **Game Production Toolbox** by Heather Maxwell Chandler
  - Also works as a great primer for those new to the game industry

### Game Design & User Experience (UX)
- **The Gamer's Brain: How Neuroscience and UX Can Impact Video Game Design** by Celia Hodent
- **Advanced Game Design A Systems Approach** by Michael Sellers
- **The Art of Game Design: A Book of Lenses** by Jesse Schell

### Engineering
- **Game Programming Patterns** by Robert Nystrom
